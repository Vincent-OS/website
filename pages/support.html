Help Support

Having a problem when using Vincent OS?
Don't worry, we're here to help! Our community is ready to assist you with any issues you may encounter.
Just reach out to us and we'll do our best to resolve your problem!



How to contact us?


Forum

The v38armageddon Forum is the best place to ask for help.
You can create a topic in the Vincent OS section and we will be happy to assist you.


GitHub

You can open an issue on GitHub to report a bug, it's the best method to report and having help of the community.


Reddit

If you prefer to use Reddit to expose your problem, you can join the subreddit r/VincentOS.


E-mail

If you prefer, you can send a mail to us at: vincent-os@v38armageddon.net
For a Security Issue, send a mail at: security@v38armageddon.net
