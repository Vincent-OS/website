Vincent OS

Standard Edition

This version ensure the best experience with Vincent OS. It is configured to work with the latest hardware and software. It is the recommended version for most users.


Recommended setup
  • CPU: AMD Athlon 3000G | Intel Core i5-1135G7
  • RAM: 4 Go (8 Go is preferable)
  • Storage: At least 30 GO



Legacy Edition

This version is specially designed for old computers. If you want to revive your old computer and wants the Vincent OS experience. This version is for you.


Recommended setup
  • CPU: AMD Sempron AM3 | Intel Celeron
  • RAM: 2 Go
  • Storage: At least 20 GO
